PSA awarness poster

A PSA poster i created to bring arenesss to Anxiety Disorder. The poster is simple, only having one colour and little text. The figure in the center depicts one symtom of anxiety, such as increased heart rate, sweating, etc, a different one on each poster. I ended up chosing the slogan "more then you think" because anxiety is not taken seriously and just thought of something you can just get over.
